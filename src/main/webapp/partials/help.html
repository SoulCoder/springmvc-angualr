About W.H.O

Wechat Hiring Optimization(W.H.O) System is a brand new Interconnected recruiting medium, It implemented the entire lifecycle from wechat advertisement (before interview) to on-boarding procedure (after join PwC), it rooted in our own PwC SDC ways of hiring, but totally changed our way of recruiting.

W.H.O System not only involves the standard operation process between HR and candidate, but also helps improving the entire interview and evaluation flow through varies of auto-generated form and humanization scoring module. Creationary work-flow solution separated candidates by their different status, which minimize the amount of unnecessary button and reduce the risk of misoperation.

In the future, W.H.O System will fuse current social-media trend and expand the recruiting pace to every potential candidate, and make our interview process more intelligent and efficient.
